How it works

A new goal every 12 weeks. Always something to chase.

The Playbook runs in rolling 12-week blocks. Each block has a specific goal, a clear finish line, and programming built to get you there. Finish one, start the next. You always know where you are and what you're building toward.

Block 1
Ground Work
Build the foundation. Compound lifts, linear progression, low reps. Every week the weight goes up. This is where the habit gets built.
Block 2
The Build
Add size and volume. You're stronger now and the program reflects it. This is where the work starts to show.
Block 3
Forge
Conditioning focus. Higher density, shorter rest, body composition shifts. This block separates the guys who showed up from the guys who committed.
Block 4
The Summit
Peak intensity. Wave loading. Personal bests. After 36 weeks of work, this is where you find out exactly what you've built.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a gym membership?

Yes. The Playbook is a gym-based strength program. You'll need access to a barbell, dumbbells, and standard gym equipment. That's what the programming is built around.

What experience level is this for?

Beginner to intermediate. If you've been training inconsistently and want a real program to follow, this is built for you. Completely new to lifting? Reach out first and we'll point you in the right direction.

How many days per week do I need?

3 primary sessions per week with 2 optional bonus days. Each session runs 45 to 60 minutes. Built around a full schedule, not a free one.

What is TrainHeroic?

It's the free app your program is delivered through. Every session is loaded with exercises, sets, reps, rest periods, and video demos. Download it, open your program, and go.

When can I start?

Right now. The Playbook runs on open enrollment, meaning there are no start dates to wait for. You join, get access immediately, and begin where the current block is at. Your welcome email will tell you exactly where you're jumping in and what to expect.

What if I join mid-block?

No problem. You start where the current team is and build from there. The programming is designed so you can enter at any point and still make progress. A new block starts every 12 weeks so you'll hit a clean entry point soon enough regardless.

What happens after the first 12 weeks?

You move into the next block. Ground Work builds into The Build, then Forge, then The Summit. Each block has a new goal and new programming. You're always chasing something and never starting from scratch.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. Email me directly to cancel whenever you want. Most people stay because the program keeps giving them something to work toward.
